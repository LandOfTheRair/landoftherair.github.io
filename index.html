What exactly is Land of the Rair?

The Land of the Rair is a game inspired by the MUDs of olde. You begin as an intrepid adventurer who joins a vast new world. You're given a piece of armor and a weapon, and from there, you get to stake your claim in the world. Kill monsters, solve puzzles, defeat those who oppose the ways of the realm, and find your place amongst the ranks of the greatest heroes to travel the land.

Online or Desktop

You can play in your web browser from anywhere. Works best in Chrome and Firefox. Desktop download coming soon.

Play your way

You can dedicate your time to growing your character either by killing monsters, or establishing yourself as a crafter in the economy.

Cost-effective

Free to play, but never pay to win. Subscribe or purchase individual boosts for your account for additional perks.
